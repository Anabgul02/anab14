DESIGN YOUR HOUSE

In house designing, lines are vital in shaping the exterior appearance, influencing the architectural style and curb appeal. They delineate the silhouette of the structure
, defining the angles of the roof, placement of windows, and arrangement of doors. These lines contribute to the overall aesthetic harmony
and visual impact of the house, creating a distinctive character that reflects the design preferences and cultural context of its inhabitants.
